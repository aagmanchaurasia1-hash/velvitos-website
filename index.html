In Every Sip Lives A Dream

We built this for
the 1AM version
of all of us.

Velvitos started with a simple belief — that great coffee and honest thickshakes should be available to everyone, at any hour. Our founder Rahul Deshmukh spent three months perfecting the brownie thickshake recipes before a single one was served to a customer. 47 batches. Only 3 made the menu.

We opened in March 2022 with twelve seats and a handwritten menu. The doors opened at 7AM and didn't close that night. They still haven't. Three years later, the menu has grown to 60+ items, but the promise hasn't changed.

"The coffee is rich and flavorful, and the menu has a great variety of drinks and snacks to choose from."
— Google Reviewer, Velvitos Customer
01
Never Close

Sleep is for the competition. Velvitos is open every hour of every day. A craving doesn't ask permission — and neither do we. 24 hours. 7 days. Always yours.

02
Never Compromise

Every brownie is baked fresh. Every thickshake is blended to order. No shortcuts, no reheating, no packet mixes. If it isn't good enough for Rahul, it doesn't reach your table.

03
Never Overcharge

A thickshake at ₹99. A full breakfast platter at ₹400. Premium doesn't have to mean expensive. We price for the person who comes every day, not just for the occasion.
